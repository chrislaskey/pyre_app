## Party Planning Checklist

### 1. Set the Basics
- **Pick a date and time** — Check with Felipe (and key guests) for availability
- **Choose a venue** — Felipe's place, your place, a restaurant, park, or rented space
- **Set a budget** — Determine how much you want to spend and whether costs are shared

### 2. Guest List & Invitations
- **Build the guest list** — Ask Felipe (if it's not a surprise) or mutual friends
- **Send invitations** — Digital (group chat, Evite, Partiful) or physical cards
- **Set an RSVP deadline** — Give yourself time to plan food/drinks quantities

### 3. Food & Drinks
- **Decide on catering approach** — Cook, order, potluck, or hire a caterer
- **Plan a menu** — Account for dietary restrictions and preferences
- **Drinks** — Alcoholic and non-alcoholic options, ice, cups
- **Cake or dessert** — A birthday cake or Felipe's favorite treat

### 4. Decorations & Atmosphere
- **Theme** — Optional, but can make planning easier (Felipe's interests?)
- **Decorations** — Balloons, banners, tablecloths, centerpieces
- **Music** — Playlist or speaker setup; consider Felipe's music taste
- **Lighting** — String lights, candles, or ambient lighting for evening events

### 5. Activities & Entertainment
- **Games or activities** — Board games, card games, karaoke, trivia
- **Photo setup** — Photo booth area or disposable cameras
- **Speeches or toasts** — Coordinate with close friends in advance

### 6. Day-of Logistics
- **Setup timeline** — Arrive early to decorate and arrange food
- **Assign helpers** — Delegate tasks (greeting guests, managing music, serving food)
- **Coordinate the surprise** — If it's a surprise party, plan the reveal moment

### 7. After the Party
- **Cleanup plan** — Trash bags, storage containers for leftovers
- **Thank-you messages** — Thank guests for coming and for any gifts
- **Share photos** — Create a shared album for everyone
